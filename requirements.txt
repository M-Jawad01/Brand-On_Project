Project Scope & Requirements
Project Name: Print on Demand System
Objective: To build a web-based platform allowing customers to order custom-sized printed materials (banners, stickers, etc.) with dynamic pricing, and an admin panel for order and service management.
Target Output: Minimal MVP website (Client Budget: 2k - 3k).
1. Core Features & Customer Experience
• Service & Client Gallery: The website will feature a gallery showcasing available services and a list of current/running clients.
• Available Services: Banner printing, LED lighting, and Sticker printing.
• Size Selection: Customers can choose from fixed PVC banner sizes (4 feet, 8 feet, 10 feet) or input custom sizes.
• Design Upload: Customers will upload their designs strictly in JPG format.
• Order Notifications: Customers expect an automated notification once an order is successfully placed.
2. Dynamic Pricing & Calculation
• Auto-Calculation: Prices will be automatically generated based on the size input (Square Feet).
• Variable Pricing: The base price varies depending on the overall size.
• Finishing Charges: Clients are willing to pay extra for finishing options (these will be added to the final auto-generated price).
• Finishing Charges: Extra charges will apply for finishing options (e.g., Eyelets, Pole Pockets, Gumming). When a customer selects these options, the additional costs will be automatically added to the final auto-generated price.
3. Admin Panel Workflow
• Dynamic Service Selection: A dedicated feature where admins can dynamically select and update which products or services are currently available on the website.
• Order Processing: Admins will update order statuses through a specific pipeline (e.g., Order Received -> Printing -> Ready for Delivery).
4. Delivery & Logistics
• Delivery Partners: Options will include RedX, Pathao, or the client''s own delivery personnel.
• Service Area: District-wise delivery must be supported.
• Emergency Options: The system needs to accommodate emergency/fast delivery requests.
Pending Questions for the Client
Pricing & Logic
• Pricing Variations: Will the pricing vary strictly based on the material, or will specific designs also affect the cost?
• Aspect Ratio Conflicts: If a customer inputs a custom size (e.g., 5x5 Square) but uploads a rectangular JPG, how should the system handle it? Should it crop the image automatically, or show a warning to the user?
Materials & Finishing Details
• Material Catalog: What specific materials are offered for the banners (e.g., Normal Banner, Star Flex, Vinyl, Cloth)?
Maintenance & Hosting
• Post-Live Plan: What is the specific plan for hosting (e.g., AWS S3 for storing uploaded high-res JPGs, Database) and ongoing maintenance?
• Operational Budget: Aside from the initial 2-3k development budget, is there a planned monthly/yearly budget for keeping the server running?
